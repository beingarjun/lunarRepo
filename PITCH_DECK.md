### Market Opportunity (Slides 2-4)
- $89B global DevOps market growing at 24.3% CAGR
- $12.8B enterprise build tools market (underserved by Moonrepo)
- Year 5 SOM: $640M at 0.5% SAM penetration

### Unique Value Proposition (Slides 5, 15)
- **Only solution** with immutable audit trails + explainability
- Local-first, zero cloud lock-in
- Open-source core + optional Lunar Cloud
- Enterprise governance by default

### Competitive Advantage (Slide 10)
- First-mover in local-first + governance
- Moonrepo: cloud lock-in, expensive ($30K-$500K/year)
- Nx: not scalable to 1000+ engineers
- Bazel: steep learning curve

- **Lunar Cloud:** $200-500/month teams + free OSS tier
- Projected Year 3 ARR: $8.5M (52 customers)
- 120% net dollar retention

### Go-to-Market (Slide 9)
- Open-source community (25% of revenue)
- Enterprise direct sales (45%)

- Self-serve freemium (10%)
